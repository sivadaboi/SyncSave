Why Use SaveSync?

Ditch proprietary clouds and take full ownership of your gaming progress.

⚡

Direct P2P LAN Sync

Sync saves directly between your systems on your home network with maximum speed. No servers or cloud limits.

📡

Secure WAN Relay

Link devices across the internet using simple shared room codes. Sync saves with remote PCs securely through end-to-end routing.

📸

Version Timeline

Every time your folder changes, SaveSync takes a safety backup snapshot. Roll back to older versions instantly if your save corrupts.

🧩

Delta Block Transfer

Optimized sync logic checks content hashes and transfers only modified blocks of files. Perfect for large emulated retro saves.

🔎

Automatic Save Scanner

Auto-detects active emulators (RPCS3, Ryujinx, PCSX2), Steam libraries, Epic Games Store, and GOG saves on your drive instantly.

🔒

Privacy-First Philosophy

No user accounts, no tracking scripts, and no telemetries. All files are compressed and stored locally on your own hardware.

Getting Started

Download the App

02

Select Your Saves

Scan your system to auto-import emulator saves, or manually select any directory path on your disk.

03

Link your systems on LAN, or input a unique room code for internet relays to synchronize directories.
